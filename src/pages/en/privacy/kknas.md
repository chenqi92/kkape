## Introduction

kknas (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is a NAS file and media management application developed by kkape. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

- NAS connection information (address, credentials) is stored locally on device only
- No third-party analytics or tracking services are used
- No advertisements are included

## Data Security

Your NAS login credentials are securely stored using the iOS/macOS system Keychain. Communication between the app and your NAS supports encrypted protocols such as HTTPS/SFTP. We do not access your data in any way.
